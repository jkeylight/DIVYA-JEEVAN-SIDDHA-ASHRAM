Offer Your Seva

Offer Your
Seva.

Not all offerings are gold. Some are hands, heart, and time.

What is Seva?

In the tantric tradition, Seva (selfless service) is equal to any monetary donation. When you offer your time, your skills, or your physical labor to the ashram, you receive the same blessings as one who offers gold.

"The hands that clean the temple floor are as sacred as the hands that light the diya."

Why Volunteer Here?

You are not just "helping out" — you are becoming part of the living Guru Parampara.

What you receive

  • ✦ Direct blessings from the Guru
  • ✦ Accommodation and simple meals during seva
  • ✦ Participation in daily rituals and sadhanas
  • ✦ Spiritual guidance and mentorship
  • ✦ A place in the ashram's daily prayers

What we receive

  • ✦ Your honest effort
  • ✦ Your presence
  • ✦ Your willingness to serve without expectation

On-Site Details

Location: Divya Jivan Siddha Ashram, Borigumma, Hradali, Odisha
Accommodation: Simple shared rooms. Basic but clean. Separate for men and women.
Food: Simple vegetarian meals provided. Cooked with devotion.
Duration: Minimum 1 week. Maximum 6 months (longer with Guru's permission).

What to bring: Comfortable clothes (modest), personal toiletries, mosquito repellent, flashlight.
What not to bring: Alcohol, non-vegetarian food, ego.
Remote Seva: Minimum 5 hours per week. Flexible scheduling.

"Do not come to serve because you have something to give. Come because you have something to receive."

— Guruji, Divya Jivan Siddha Ashram
