Sarp Vidya — 3-Year Programme

Sarp Vidya — the science of the serpent — is one of the oldest and most complete esoteric sciences in the Indian tradition. This three-year residential programme covers: Naga mantra and stotra, Sarp Sadhana methods, serpent deity worship, antidote kriyas, and the complete philosophy of Naga consciousness.

This course is transmitted only in person and only through direct Guru initiation. The next cohort begins by appointment.
